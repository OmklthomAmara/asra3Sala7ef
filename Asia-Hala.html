Volunteer in Asia

Volunteer in Asia with Asra3 sala7ef- Our Volunteer abroad organization. We provide safe, trusted and affordable programs overseas, with a wide range pf volunteer projects in Bali, Cambodia, India, Sri Lanka, Thailand & Maldives.

Alsla7ef Asia is located in various settings across the continent, including Cambodia’s quaint village of Bakod and the bustling capital of Vietnam, Hanoi. PMGY programs start on two dedicated start dates every month. Our projects in Asia run throughout the year and volunteers can participate in our programs from 1-24 weeks. Volunteers in Asia are provided with an extensive pre-departure support service, airport pick-up, orientation, meals, accommodation and local support.
